Standards

Standards are very important as they lend uniformity to the areas of creation and consumption i.e. for developers and visitors of websites/machines or programs reading the page.

When developers follow standards, it is easier for them to know what to expect from a particular piece of code that they write and also to understand each other's code easily.

Standards are also important from a machine learning perspective, as they make it a lot easier for machines/other programs to read and process information.

For example, if standards are followed, the uniformity enables ease of access to various users.
